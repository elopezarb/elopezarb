## Selected projects

### [MXN Benchmark Transition Research](https://github.com/elopezarb/tesina-transicion-tasa-referencia)

Research and implementation examining the transition from 28-day TIIE to TIIE de Fondeo and its implications for Mexico's interest-rate swap market.

Topics include benchmark reform, overnight-indexed rates, swap valuation, yield-curve construction, and monetary-policy transmission.

### [Flash Anzan](https://github.com/elopezarb/flash_anzan)

An algorithm for generating mental-arithmetic exercises under the level-specific rules of Soroban. Developed as part of a broader educational platform for abacus training.
